Wiegand Gymnasium has been outfitted to be the home of varsity basketball and volleyball for the Tartans while construction of the Highmark Center for Health, Wellness and Athletics takes place through the spring of 2024. The arena includes seating for 1,000 fans and is located inside the Jared L. Cohon University Center with the main entrance on the east side of the building.

Recreation Policy

Must have a valid CMU ID to use the space

Intramural and club sports activities also use Wiegand Gym.
